# Data boosts crime stories

**By Megan Christensen, IRE and NICAR**

For the resourceful journalist using computer-assisted reporting, there are as many great criminal justice stories waiting to be written as there are Law & Order reruns on cable.

Stories about local and national crime trends often begin with the FBI's Uniform Crime Reports. The data is compiled from voluntary reports submitted by city, county, state and national law enforcement agencies. Roughly 95 percent of all police agencies report.
