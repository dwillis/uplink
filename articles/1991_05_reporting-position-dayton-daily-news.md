# Reporting Position in Ohio

The Dayton Daily News is accepting applications for a projects reporting position. Candidates must have demonstrated investigative reporting skills and an aptitude for using personal computers as a reporting tool.

In the past year we have done computer-assisted reporting projects on discrimination in court sentencing, campaign finance and safety in the workplace.

We have sent reporters through courses at the MICAR and have worked with faculty members of the National Institute for Advanced Reporting at Indiana University. Applicants should send resumes and clips to Jim Ripley, executive metro editor at:
