#### Government continues infotech push

Journalists who have yet to go online will fall even farther behind during the next few years if the federal government follows recommendations to give all its employees access to electronic mail by 1997.

In a report issued last month, the Government E-Mail Task Force also advised agencies to provide outside access to their networks and publish their e-mail addresses by the end of this year.
