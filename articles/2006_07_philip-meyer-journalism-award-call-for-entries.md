# Philip Meyer Journalism Award — Call for Entries

**Sponsored by**

**National Institute for Computer-Assisted Reporting**
*a joint program of IRE and the Missouri School of Journalism*

and

**the Knight Chair in Journalism**
*at the Walter Cronkite School of Journalism and Mass Communication, Arizona State University*

The contest recognizes the best journalism done using social science research methods.

All entries are placed in IRE's Resource Center story library so IRE members can learn from others' triumphs and troubles.

**POSTMARK DEADLINE: October 31, 2006**
