# Starting small

By David Donald, *IRE and NICAR*

What's next?

That's the question I face most often when I finish teaching an introductory session of computer-assisted reporting to journalists from small news organizations.

What they're often really asking is simple: How do I go back to a small- or medium-sized newsroom that has never done CAR?

Sad to say, even today some larger newsrooms exist without doing much CAR. But for reporters in smaller organizations...
