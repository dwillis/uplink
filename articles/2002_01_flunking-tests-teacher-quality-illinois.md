# Flunking Tests

**By Becky Beaupre, *Chicago Sun-Times***

Teachers who had flunked at least one test of teacher competence taught more than 180,000 Illinois schoolchildren last year. Some of these teachers failed exams – including a Basic Skills test written at an eighth- or ninth-grade level – five or 10 times before passing. Some never passed.

But before the Chicago Sun-Times started poring over teacher test score data earlier this year, nobody but the individual test takers knew it – not even the state agency that kept the data.
