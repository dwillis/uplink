## Stories from the IRE Resource Center

**"Boom on the Beach,"** *USA Today*
This series examined economic growth and development along the East and Gulf coasts. The series revealed that in spite of many natural threats, such as long-term hurricane risk, rising sea level, fragile sands and erosion, "growth pressure keeps building." The journalists used data from annual county-level disaster declarations from Federal Emergency Management Agency, annual mean sea-level data from the NOAA and nationwide municipal building permit data. The story was based on analysis and mapping using Microsoft Access, Excel and ESRI ArcView. (Story No. 17357)
