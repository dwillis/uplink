# Correction

There was an error in the formula for February's lead story about the Gini coefficient.

The correct formula is:

```
Gini coefficient=1 - SUM
((Xi-Xj)*(Yi+Yj))
where:
X is the cumulative
proportion of households;
Y is the cumulative
proportion of income;
i is a particular income
category;
j is i-1, or the preceding
income category.
*Xi, Xj, Yi, Yj must equal
one or less
```
